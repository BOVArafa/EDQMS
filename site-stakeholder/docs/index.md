# Welcome

This site is the stakeholder-facing documentation for the **EDQMS project** — an Event Driven Quality Management System being developed by Neun Design for Siemens Energy's Power Transformer Repair Global Engineering Hub.

Whether you are a project sponsor, a process owner, an operations manager, or a decision-maker evaluating the progress of this initiative, this site gives you everything you need to understand what we are building, why it matters, and where we stand today.

## What is EDQMS?

EDQMS stands for **Event Driven Quality Management System**. It is a methodology and data architecture that enables an organisation to manage quality operations reactively and proactively — not through calendar-based audits alone, but through structured responses to the events that occur in daily operations.

In practical terms, it means that every time something significant happens in the engineering workflow — a customer request arrives, a technical deviation is detected, a handover between teams takes place — the system knows what to do, who is responsible, and how to execute it. All of it is traceable, auditable, and aligned with ISO 9001:2015.

## Why this project exists

The Siemens Energy Power Transformer Repair Global Engineering Hub was established to serve business units across multiple regions. When the hub launched, its teams faced a challenge familiar to any newly formed global organisation: the knowledge of how to execute work lived in people's heads, not in a shared and structured system. Process maps existed, but they did not connect to the day-to-day reality of executing engineering tasks across international interfaces.

This project was initiated to address that gap — to answer the question that surfaced during the Discovery phase:

> *"When something happens, will we know when to act, what is required, and how to execute it all?"*

EDQMS is the answer to that question.
